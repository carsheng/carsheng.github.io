Biography

Caroline was born in San Francisco, CA, where she attended San Francisco University High School. She is a sophomore at Tufts University majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Chinese. At Tufts Caroline is a member of Women in Computer Science, Tufts Gaming Hub, and is also involved in the Tufts theater tech community.

In her free time Caroline enjoys reading fantasy novels, playing Hearthstone, and trying to cook.
